# Product research brief - 2026-07-13

Do not change business code until the first three judging dimensions are evidence-backed.

## Questions

1. Scene value: Is the problem real, for whom, in what exact situation, how often, with what loss, and what do they use today?
2. Commercial potential: Who pays, why, what changes a service into a product or repeatable solution, and what evidence would justify continuing?
3. Innovation: What is the old workflow, what do current AI/Agent products already do, and what product difference can be demonstrated without false novelty claims?

## Current candidate causal chain

```text
new customer information
-> interpret it with company context
-> identify affected quote / commitment / project / task
-> propose an action for approval
-> execute
-> verify the result
-> update company memory
```

This chain is a hypothesis, not an approved product specification.

## Evidence rules

- Primary sources and original research first.
- Record sample, method, date, limits, and counter-evidence.
- Vendor research is conflict-marked.
- No market, willingness-to-pay, or novelty claim without evidence.
- User self-report is founder evidence until backed by real artifacts or behavior.
