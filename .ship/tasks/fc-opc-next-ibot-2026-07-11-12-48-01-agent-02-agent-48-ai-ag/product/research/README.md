## Evidence discipline

- Public prices prove paid supply and price anchors, not willingness to pay.
- Vendor surveys are conflict-marked and do not prove causal effects.
- Broad knowledge-worker data does not become solo-operator evidence by analogy.
- “No reviewed product found” is not a market-blank or global-first claim.
- Founder experience is one-user evidence until supported by real artifacts and repeated behavior.
